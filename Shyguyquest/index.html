What is Shyguyquest?

After watching many videos by the popular indie-game YouTuber, Shyguymask, you eventually find out that he has a public Discord server. As a motivated fan, you join instantly. After reading (most) of the rules and info, you set one goal: to level up to the highest rank and dominate the server! However, there are a lot of obstacles ahead, so you must push through different members, channels, games, references, and above all, the mods. Will you succeed in becoming Shyguymask’s #1 fan?
